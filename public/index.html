⛔
High-Risk Asset Warning
Read before adding Crypto to your Financial Plan
💸
30% Flat Tax on Every Gain
No slab rate, no LTCG benefit. A 15% gross return becomes ~10.5% after tax — similar to large-cap equity but with far higher risk.
🏦
1% TDS on Every Transaction
Deducted on transactions above ₹10,000. Continuously reduces usable capital, even on losing trades.
🚫
No Loss Set-Off Allowed
Crypto losses cannot offset gains from any source — not even other crypto. Each coin is taxed independently.
📉
Extreme Volatility + Regulatory Risk
50–80% drawdowns are common. Bitcoin fell 77% in 2022. India's crypto regulation is unsettled — holdings could become illiquid.

By adding crypto, you confirm you have read and understood the risks above. Projections are illustrative only.
