📖 What is Coffee Can Investing?

Coined by Robert Kirby (1984) and popularised in India by Saurabh Mukherjea (Marcellus Investment Managers): lock high-quality businesses in a "coffee can" and forget them for 10 years. The philosophy demands companies with ≥10% revenue CAGR, ≥15% ROCE consistently over a decade, negligible debt, positive free cash flow every year, and stable promoter holdings — moat businesses that compound wealth without active management.
