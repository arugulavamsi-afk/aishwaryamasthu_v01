📌 Safe Withdrawal Rate Guide for Indian Retirees
🎯 4% Rule (US origin)
Withdraw 4% Year 1, adjust for inflation. Risky for India — higher inflation & sequence-of-returns risk.
✅ 3% Rule (India-safe)
Withdraw 3% Year 1. Corpus survives 35+ years with 8% blended return — suits India's 5–7% inflation.
💡 Dynamic SWR
Withdraw less in downturns (2–2.5%), more in booms (4–5%). Most resilient with bucket strategy.
