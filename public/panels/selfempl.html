⚠️ Late GST Filing Penalty
₹50/day (₹20 for nil return) + 18% p.a. interest on unpaid GST from due date. For 3R filers, GSTR-3B due by 20th of next month. GSTR-1 by 11th. Missing both = double penalty.
💡 GST Cash-Flow Tip
Never mix GST collected with operating funds. Open a dedicated GST escrow account. Transfer net GST payable on the day of invoicing — not on the 20th. This eliminates the monthly cash crunch completely.

📚 Self-Employed Tax Rules You Must Know
✅ 44AD: Who Qualifies?
Any business (not professional) with turnover ≤₹3Cr (if 95%+ receipts are digital). Deemed profit = 6% (digital) or 8% (cash) of turnover. No books of accounts needed. Cannot claim deductions separately.
✅ 44ADA: Professionals
Doctors, lawyers, CAs, architects, engineers, consultants with gross receipts ≤₹75L. Deemed profit = 50% of receipts. No books required if opting in. Very powerful for high-margin professionals.
⚠️ When Regular Books Beat Presumptive
If your actual profit margin is below 6%/8% (trading, thin margins), maintaining regular books and claiming actual profit + all deductions saves more tax. Use the comparison tab to decide.
🔒 Opting Out Restriction
Once you opt out of presumptive scheme, you cannot re-opt for 5 years. Think carefully before switching. If you opt in and your actual profit is lower than deemed, you still pay tax on deemed profit.
