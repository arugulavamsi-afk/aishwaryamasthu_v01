📌 Gratuity Rules Every Employee Must Know
✅ Eligibility
Minimum 5 years of continuous service with the same employer. Gratuity is legally mandatory under Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972.
📅 Partial Year Rule
If you've worked >6 months in the final year, it counts as a full year. E.g. 7 years 8 months → treated as 8 years for gratuity calculation.
🧾 Tax Exemption
Gratuity up to ₹25L is fully tax-free u/s 10(10) for private sector employees. Any amount above ₹25L is taxable at your income-tax slab rate.
⏰ Payment Timeline
Employer must pay within 30 days of leaving. If delayed, they owe simple interest at 10% p.a. from the due date. You can file a complaint with Labour Commissioner.
