🗓️ Your 90-Day Action Plan
📚 What Moves Your Score
Payment History 35%
Never miss a due date. Even 1 missed EMI can drop score by 80–100 points and stays on report for 3 years.
Credit Utilisation 30%
Keep below 30% of total limit. Below 10% is ideal. High utilisation signals financial stress to lenders.
Credit Age 15%
Longer history = better score. Never close your oldest credit card — even if unused, keep it alive.
Credit Mix 10%
Having both secured (home/car loan) and unsecured (credit card) credit improves score.
New Enquiries 10%
Each loan/card application triggers a hard enquiry. Multiple enquiries in 6 months signal desperation to lenders.
🛡️ How to Dispute CIBIL Errors
1.Download your free CIBIL report at cibil.com (1 free/year). Also check Experian, CRIF, and Equifax — errors may differ.
2.Look for: wrong personal details, accounts you don't recognise, closed accounts showing as open, incorrect loan amounts, duplicate entries.
3.Raise a dispute at cibil.com/dispute. CIBIL must resolve it within 30 days under RBI guidelines.
4.If the lender doesn't respond in 30 days, escalate to RBI's Integrated Ombudsman at cms.rbi.org.in — completely free.
5.Errors corrected within 30–45 days; score can jump 20–80 points if a major negative entry is removed.
