📌 Quick How-To Guide
🏛️ EPF Nomination
Login → EPFO Unified Portal → e-Nomination → Add family member → Submit. Free & takes 5 min.
📊 MF Nomination
CAMS Online or KFintech portal → Account Services → Nomination. Valid across all MF folios in one go.
📋 Writing a Will
Typed or handwritten. Sign in presence of 2 witnesses (not beneficiaries). Register at Sub-Registrar. ~₹200–₹500 stamp duty.
🔄 When to Review
After marriage · Divorce · Birth of child · Death of nominee · Major asset purchase. Review every 3–5 years minimum.
