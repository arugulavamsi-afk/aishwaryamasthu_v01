⚠️ Benchmark Methodology — Please Read

What we use: Alpha, Beta, Sharpe, and Sortino are calculated against a category-appropriate index fund NAV (e.g., Nippon Nifty 100 for Large Cap, Nifty 500 proxy for Large & Mid Cap, Motilal Nifty Midcap 150 for Mid Cap). We use fund NAVs because the AMFI data API does not expose raw index levels directly.

Why this matters: Every fund has a SEBI-mandated benchmark that may differ from our category proxy. A Large Cap fund benchmarked to Nifty 50 will show higher alpha vs our Nifty 100 proxy; vice versa for funds benchmarked to Nifty 100. The proxy introduces a small tracking error (typically ±0.05–0.30% p.a.) that can shift alpha by ±0.1–0.5%. Gilt funds currently use an overnight proxy — this is a known gap; alpha figures for Gilt funds are not meaningful.

Most reliable metrics: Rolling Hit Rate and Rolling Avg Return are benchmark-independent — they measure a fund's absolute consistency and are the most reliable signals here. CAGR and Std Dev are also unaffected by benchmark choice.

Before investing: Always verify Alpha and Sharpe against the fund's own factsheet (benchmarked to its SEBI-mandated index). This tool is for relative screening and comparison — not a substitute for the fund's official performance disclosure.
