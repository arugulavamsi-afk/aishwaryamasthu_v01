Always Compare Against the Right Benchmark

A Large Cap fund must be benchmarked against the Nifty 100 — not Nifty 50 which covers only the top 50 stocks. A fund adding 2% Alpha over its benchmark is excellent. But if a fund picks a weak benchmark to inflate numbers, the metrics look misleadingly good. Always check the benchmark listed in the fund's factsheet before trusting any metric.

Large Cap → Nifty 100 (top 100) Large & Mid Cap → Nifty LargeMidcap 250 Mid Cap → Nifty Midcap 150 Multi Cap → Nifty 500 Multicap 50:25:25 Flexi Cap → Nifty 500 Gilt → Long G-Sec Index
