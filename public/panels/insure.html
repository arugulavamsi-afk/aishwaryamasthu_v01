📌 India Insurance Adequacy Rules (Post-COVID Baseline)
🎯 HLV for Term
10–15× income minus liquid assets. Your savings reduce the gap — family already holds that wealth.
🏥 Base Floater
Min ₹10L family floater. Don't rely on employer cover — it ends when you leave. Separate policy is essential.
⭐ Super Top-Up
Add ₹25L super top-up above ₹5L deductible. ICU post-COVID: ₹50K/day. Target total: ₹35L+.
🎗️ Critical Illness
India: 14L new cancer cases/year. CI pays a lump sum — hospital bills + lost income + recovery. Target ₹25–50L.
♿ Disability
1-in-4 chance of disability before 65. Income replacement corpus = 10× annual income. Most Indians have zero cover.
👴 Parent Floater
Senior citizen floaters cost ₹25K–65K/yr. Target ₹5L floater + ₹20L top-up for each parent pair.
