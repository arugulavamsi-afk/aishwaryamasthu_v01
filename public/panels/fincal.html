💸 Advance Tax: Cost of Missing
Miss an advance tax instalment? You owe 1% interest per month under Sec 234B/234C. On ₹1L tax liability, that's ₹12,000 extra per year — for no reason. Pay quarterly: Jun 15, Sep 15, Dec 15, Mar 15.
📋 ITR: Beyond the Deadline
Miss Jul 31? You can file a belated return by Dec 31, but you'll pay ₹1,000–₹5,000 late fee (₹5,000 if income above ₹5L) + 1% interest/month on unpaid tax. Losses also cannot be carried forward.
🏛️ PPF: The April Rule
Deposit before 5th of each month to earn interest for that month. Deposit before April 5 to earn interest for the entire April — a free month of 7.1% p.a. on up to ₹1.5L. Most people miss this.
📈 ELSS: The March Trap
Invest in ELSS before March 31 to claim 80C for that FY. But March mutual fund servers crash — payment fails but units aren't allotted in time. Invest in Jan–Feb. Rushed March investments cause ₹1,000s in lost deductions.
