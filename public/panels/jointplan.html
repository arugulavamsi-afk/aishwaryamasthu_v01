📌 Why Joint Planning Beats Individual Planning in India
✅ Double LTCG Exemption
Each individual has ₹1.25L/yr LTCG exemption on equity MF gains. A couple together can harvest ₹2.5L tax-free annually — that's ₹15,625 saved every year at 12.5% tax rate.
📅 Split Higher-Tax Investments
Route FD interest, rental income, and dividend-paying instruments to the lower-tax-slab spouse. If one partner is in 30% slab and another in 20%, this saves 10% tax on that income.
🧾 Double 80C + NPS
Both spouses can independently claim ₹1.5L under 80C + ₹50K NPS deduction = ₹4L total deductions/yr under old regime, potentially saving ₹1.2L–₹1.6L in tax annually.
⚡ Goal Parallelism
Two incomes allow parallel goal funding. While Partner 1 SIPs for child's education, Partner 2 builds the home down-payment corpus. No single goal starves the other.
