📌 PPF Key Rules
🔒 Lock-in
15 years. Extendable in 5-yr blocks indefinitely. Cannot close before maturity except in extreme cases.
💸 Partial Withdrawal
From Year 7 onwards: up to 50% of balance at end of Year 4 or Year 6 (whichever is lower). Once per year.
🏦 Loan Against PPF
Year 3 to Year 6: up to 25% of balance at end of Year 2 (or Year N-2). Interest = PPF rate + 1%.
🧾 Tax Benefits
EEE status: contribution (80C up to ₹1.5L), interest earned, and maturity amount — all fully tax-free.

📌 NPS Key Rules
🎯 At Retirement (60)
Withdraw 60% as tax-free lumpsum. Remaining 40% must buy annuity for monthly pension.
🧾 Tax Deductions
80C: up to ₹1.5L. Extra 80CCD(1B): ₹50,000 more. Employer contribution u/s 80CCD(2): 10% of basic, no cap.
💸 Partial Withdrawal
Up to 25% after 3 years for specific purposes (education, marriage, home, illness). Max 3 times.
⚠ Annuity Taxation
Monthly pension from annuity is taxable at slab rate. Only lumpsum 60% is tax-free. Plan accordingly.
